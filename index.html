Yang Yang

Chinese Actor

Here's a timeline of Yang Yang's Career:

  • 2007 - he was chosen to play the lead role Jia Baoyu by director Li Shaohong in The Dream of Red Mansions
  • 2011 - Yang featured in The Founding of a Party, a patriotic tribute detailing the process of establishing the Chinese Communist Party
  • 2014 - Yang ended his contract of 7 years with his company Rosat Entertainment.
  • 2015 - Yang started to gain recognition for his performance in The Left Ear, a coming-of-age film which is also the directorial debut of Alec Su
  • 2016 - Yang appeared in CCTV New Year's Gala for the first time, where his song item "Father and Son" alongside Tong Tiexin was voted the most popular program
  • 2017 - Yang starred alongside Liu Yifei in romantic fantasy film Once Upon a Time
  • 2018 - Yang starred in the fantasy action drama Martial Universe.
  • 2019 - Yang played the leading role Ye Xiu in the eSports drama The King's Avatar.
  • 2020 - Yang starred in action movie Vanguard alongside Jackie Chan.
  • 2022 - Yang starred in the fantasy romance drama titled Who Rules the World opposite Zhao Lusi, based on the novel by Qing Lengyue
  • 2023 - He played the lead role as a firefighter, alongside Wang Chu Ran.
